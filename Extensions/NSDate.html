  • TimeFormat

    TimeFormat is an enum for formatting timestamps.

    Given an hour, minute, and second, the time will be formatted in one of four formats. - HourMinuteMilitary (e.g. 19:41) - HourMinutePeriod (e.g. 07:41 PM) - HourMinuteSecondMilitary (e.g. 19:41:30) - HourMinuteSecondPeriod (e.g. 07:41:30 PM)

  • time(_:)

    The time, formatted with a specified TimeFormat.

    Format options: - HourMinuteMilitary (e.g. 19:41) - HourMinutePeriod (e.g. 07:41 PM) - HourMinuteSecondMilitary (e.g. 19:41:30) - HourMinuteSecondPeriod (e.g. 07:41:30 PM)

    Default format is HourMinuteMilitary.
